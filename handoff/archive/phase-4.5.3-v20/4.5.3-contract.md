# Step 4.5.3 Contract -- Reconciliation overlay (paper-live vs parallel OOS backtest)

**Drafted:** 2026-04-16
**Parent:** `handoff/current/phase-4.5-contract.md`

## Hypothesis
A frictionless shadow backtest replaying paper trades against yfinance adjusted closes lets us detect execution drift, stale-signal bugs, or environment regressions early. When the paper and shadow curves diverge >5%, something is wrong and should block Go-Live (4.5.4).

## Success criteria (from masterplan 4.5.3)
1. parallel_oos_backtest_runs_on_same_signals_as_live -- shadow simulator consumes `paper_trades` rows verbatim (same quantity, ticker, action, date).
2. equity_curves_aligned_by_date -- shadow NAV sampled at each `paper_snapshots.snapshot_date`; per-date series returned.
3. divergence_pct_computed_and_alerted_when_gt_5pct -- `|paper_nav - shadow_nav| / shadow_nav * 100`; `alert` flag true when latest > 5%.
4. chart_overlays_render_in_frontend -- `PaperReconciliationChart` renders both lines + divergence on right Y-axis + alert banner.
5. evaluator_critique_pass.

## Design
- `backend/services/reconciliation.py` (new): `_shadow_nav_curve` replays FIFO trades with yfinance adj-close fills; `compute_reconciliation` aligns curves by date.
- `backend/api/paper_trading.py`: new `GET /reconciliation` endpoint; 600s cache TTL (expensive yfinance round-trip).
- `frontend/src/components/PaperReconciliationChart.tsx` (new): Recharts dual-axis line chart + 4-card summary + alert banner.
- `frontend/src/app/paper-trading/page.tsx`: new "Reality gap" tab lazily loads reconciliation on first click.
- `frontend/src/lib/api.ts` / `types.ts`: `getPaperReconciliation` + `PaperReconciliation*` types.

## Anti-patterns guarded
- Shadow uses adjusted close, not yfinance raw close -- avoids corp-action drift.
- yfinance errors swallowed per-ticker (not blocking); empty price dict is valid state.
- Cache TTL 600s (not 120s) -- yfinance is the expensive dep.
- No mutable writes: reconciliation is read-only (no BQ inserts, no side effects).

## Out of scope
- Persisting per-cycle reconciliation rows to BQ (could add `paper_reconciliation` table in a later iteration).
- Slippage / txcost modeling in shadow -- intentionally frictionless; that's the whole point of the reality-gap check.
- Weekend/holiday gap handling -- yfinance history naturally skips non-trading days.

## Risk
- yfinance rate limits across many tickers -- cache + endpoint-level TTL mitigate.
- Early-history snapshots may not have matching yfinance data -- falls back to paper fill price.
